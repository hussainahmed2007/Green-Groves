Gardening Tips and Tricks

Healthy Soil

Use nutrient-rich soil to ensure optimal plant growth. Test soil regularly for quality.

Watering Timing

Water plants early morning to reduce evaporation. Keep soil moist but not waterlogged.

Mulching

Use organic mulch to retain moisture and prevent weeds. It also enriches the soil.

Crop Rotation

Change planting spots yearly to maintain soil nutrients and avoid pests.

Regular Pruning

Trim plants to promote healthy growth and remove dead parts regularly.

Companion Planting

Pair plants to enhance growth and deter pests naturally.

Harvest Timing

Pick vegetables and fruits when ripe to enjoy the best flavor and nutrition.

Native Plants

Grow native species for lower maintenance and better adaptability to local climates.

Tool Maintenance

Keep gardening tools clean and sharp to prevent plant diseases.

Grouped Plants

Plant species with similar water needs together to simplify care.

Raised Beds

Build raised beds for better drainage and improved soil conditions.

Composting

Start composting to recycle organic waste and create nutrient-rich fertilizer.
